About me

With all the constant changes in knowledge, I look to see how far I can challenge myself with experimentation to try to understand what makes up the Internet.


Most things that I do involve trying to figure stuff out on my own; I love to study and aim to remain a life-long learner. That is why I chose Northwestern University to teach me from the basics on how to make a website and web applications.


In my own personal time, I run my own photography business, where my interest lies in editing my photos to tell the story that I want them to tell. I find fishing to be very interesting, and plan to learn how to fish one day soon. I spend the most time with my computer, something I fell in love with a very long time ago. (Will tell you the story one day.)


I play video games that offer very high levels of customization, push players to find pieces to the puzzle, and that most of all prioritize working as a collaborative team. I love to spend some family time and relax wth my lovely family.
